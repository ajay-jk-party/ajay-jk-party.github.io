Logistics

Getting there and accommodation

Getting There

From Frankfurt Airport or Frankfurt Hbf

Take a regional train (RE or RB) from Frankfurt (Main) Hbf directly to Darmstadt Hauptbahnhof. Trains run frequently (about every 15 to 30 minutes) and the trip takes around 15 to 25 minutes depending on the train.

Tip: You can use an RMV ticket or a Deutschlandticket for this route.

From Darmstadt Hauptbahnhof to the Venue

Once at Darmstadt Hbf, you have a few options:

  • Tram or Bus: Take any tram or bus from Darmstadt Hbf to Rhein-Neckar-Strasse, then cross the road and take tram 6, 7, or 8. Get off at Marienh√∂he stop.
  • Taxi or Rideshare: A short taxi ride from the station to the venue takes about 10 minutes and costs around 15 euros.
  • Walking: The venue is about 4 km from the station, approximately 40 to 50 minutes on foot.

Parking

Street parking is available near the venue. Parking meters are nearby but availability is subject to demand.

Useful Apps

  • RMV app (RMVgo): For real-time trip planning and ticket purchase (train, bus, and tram in one).
  • Deutsche Bahn app: For specific train times and booking.
  • OpenStreetMap: For walking and driving directions.

Accommodation

For those coming from outside Darmstadt, accommodation details will be updated on your personal page and will be discussed and confirmed in individual calls.
