Getting There

Best public transport route (train + local stop)

Step 1: Train from Frankfurt Hbf to Darmstadt Hauptbahnhof

  • Take a regional train (DB Regio / RE / RB) from Frankfurt (Main) Hbf directly to Darmstadt Hauptbahnhof.
  • These run very frequently (about every 30 minutes or more) and the trip takes around 17 to 20 minutes.

Tip: You can use an RMV ticket (for example a single ticket or a Deutschlandticket) for this route if you are in the RMV area.

Step 2: From Darmstadt Hauptbahnhof to Bessungen

Once at Darmstadt Hbf, you have a few good local options to reach the Bessungen neighborhood:

Option A (bus)

  • Several buses stop near Darmstadt Hbf, including lines like 693, AIR, FM, WE1, etc., connecting from Darmstadt Hbf toward Lincoln Siedlung.

Option B (tram)

  • You can also take a tram or light rail from Darmstadt Hbf toward Bessungen (tram lines 3 or 8 are common options in Darmstadt).

Option C (walk / short taxi)

  • Lincoln Siedlung is quite walkable from the Hauptbahnhof (about 20-30 minutes on foot depending on your exact destination).
  • Or take a short taxi / rideshare if you have luggage or want a direct drop.

Tools that make this easy

  • RMV app (RMVgo) for real-time trip planning and ticket purchase (train + bus + tram in one).
  • Deutsche Bahn app or website for specific train times.

Summary travel idea

  1. Train from Frankfurt Hbf to Darmstadt Hbf (~17-20 min).
  2. Local bus/tram from Darmstadt Hbf to Lincoln Siedlung (~10-20 min).

This is the simplest and most frequent public transport option.
